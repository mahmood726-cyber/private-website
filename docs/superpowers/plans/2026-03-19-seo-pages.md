## Content Writing Rules (for all tasks)

Every article must follow these Quranic storytelling principles:

1. **Lead with the lesson** — state the insight, then prove it
2. **Direct address** — "you", "your heart", "have you ever..."
3. **Real data as parables** — cite real studies narratively, never fabricate
4. **Rhetorical questions** — provoke reflection, not anxiety
5. **Hope after hardship** — arc: fear → knowledge → relief → action
6. **Economy of language** — no filler, short paragraphs, white space
7. **Nature analogies** — nervous system as river, heart as rhythm responding to life
8. **The turning point** — one moment of shifted understanding per article
9. **The reader as participant** — "you are already doing the hardest part"

**Evidence rules:**
- Only cite studies mentioned in the OpenPalp booklet or spec (Springer Nature 2024, Miner SLEEP 2016, JACC 2021 UK Biobank, PubMed 2015 BMI study, CAST 1989)
- Never fabricate statistics
- Hedge appropriately ("studies suggest", "in many patients")

**HTML rules:**
- Use `&pound;` `&mdash;` `&ndash;` `&rsquo;` `&ldquo;` `&rdquo;` for special characters
- Address: 40-44 The Broadway, Wimbledon, London SW19 1RQ
- Price: £49.99 clinic, £49.99 programme, £99.98 total
- Each article ends with CTA box + 2-3 related article links
- All pages must have unique `<title>`, `<meta description>`, `<canonical>`
