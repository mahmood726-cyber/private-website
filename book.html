Cancellation & Refund Policy

More than 48 hours before appointment: Full refund, no questions asked.

24–48 hours before appointment: 50% refund. The booking slot will be released for another patient.

Less than 24 hours / no-show: No refund. The full fee is retained.

CardioTrack device hold: The £200 hold on your card is released within 2 working days of the device being returned and inspected. If the device is lost or damaged, the hold may be captured in full or in part.
