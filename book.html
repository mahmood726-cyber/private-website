Book Your Appointment

This clinic is exclusively for adults with low-risk palpitations. If you have fainting, chest pain, severe breathlessness, or feel acutely unwell, please seek urgent medical attention instead. All appointments take place on Friday evenings in Wimbledon, SW19.

Part 1

Clinic Appointment

£50

A focused 30-minute assessment for low-risk palpitations. We review your symptoms, triggers, and risk factors to confirm suitability for the pathway.

  • 30-minute face-to-face appointment
  • Focused palpitation and rhythm assessment
  • Trigger and risk factor review
  • ECG if part of the service model
  • Confirmation of pathway suitability
  • Clear explanation and next steps
Part 2

Monitoring & 6-Week Programme

£50

Structured ECG monitoring and a guided improvement programme covering breathing, triggers, sleep, activity, and symptom response.

  • 2-week Kardia ECG monitoring
  • Review of submitted recordings
  • Extended monitoring if clinically appropriate
  • 4-page patient guide
  • Breathing, activity, sleep, and trigger protocols
  • Light-touch reminders and support
  • Review and explanation of findings
  • Onward referral if significant findings arise
