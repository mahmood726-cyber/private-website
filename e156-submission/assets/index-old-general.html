A warm welcome to our cardiac clinic

Our clinic offers cardiology assessment in a calm, unhurried setting. With experience working on the cardiology consultant rota at the Royal Free Hospital and Barnet Hospital, we bring extensive experience in managing common cardiac conditions in private practice.

We focus on low-risk cardiac conditions where timely cardiology input can make a real difference to your care.

  • GMC Registered
  • MRCP (UK)
  • Fully Insured

Dr Mahmood is a Specialist in Cardiology working on the consultant rota at the Royal Free Hospital and Barnet Hospital. He holds MRCP(UK) and is GMC-registered (GMC no. 6071047). He does not hold a Certificate of Completion of Training (CCT) and is not a consultant cardiologist. This clinic is not affiliated with or endorsed by the Royal Free London NHS Foundation Trust or Barnet Hospital.

Our facilities

What we offer

12-lead ECG and full clinical assessment. We provide a thorough cardiology consultation, interpret your ECG, review your symptoms and history, and give you a clear management plan. A written report is sent to your GP with your consent.

What we do not offer

We do not offer echocardiography, Holter monitoring (24-hour tape), ambulatory blood pressure monitoring, CT cardiac imaging, MRI, or exercise stress testing. If any of these investigations are clinically indicated, we will refer you to the appropriate NHS or private service.
