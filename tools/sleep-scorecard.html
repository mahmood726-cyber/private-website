Sleep Hygiene Scorecard

Sleep is one of the strongest predictors of heart rhythm stability. The UK Biobank study of 403,000 people found that disrupted sleep patterns significantly increase arrhythmia risk. Rate your current habits below.

I wake up at the same time every day, including weekends. This is me
I use my phone or tablet in bed before sleep. This is me
I avoid caffeine after 2pm. This is me
I often fall asleep with the TV on. This is me
My bedroom is cool, dark, and quiet. This is me
I go to bed at very different times on weeknights versus weekends. This is me
I have a wind-down routine in the 30 minutes before bed. This is me

Personalised suggestions

Record your sleep observations in your OpenPalp booklet (Week 4) and discuss them at your results appointment. This scorecard does not store any data.
